Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Aloha Rainbow ("the App") is a rainbow forecast app for the Hawaiian Islands. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

  • Approximate Location — Used to automatically detect which Hawaiian island you are on, so the app can show the relevant forecast and send you notifications for that island only. Location is not stored beyond what is needed to complete registration.
  • Device ID — A randomly generated identifier stored on your device. It is used to deliver push notifications and to prevent duplicate registrations. It is not linked to your name, email, or any other personal information.

How We Use This Data

The data above is used solely to provide core app functionality: island detection and push notification delivery. It is not used for advertising, analytics, or any form of tracking.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • AWS SNS — Used to deliver push notifications to your device.
  • Open-Meteo — Used to retrieve weather forecast data. No personal data is sent to Open-Meteo.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties beyond the services listed above.

Data Retention

Your device ID and approximate location are stored only as long as you have the App installed and notifications enabled. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.
