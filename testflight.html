Aloha Rainbow — Beta Test Guide

How to install the app via TestFlight and what to try first

1
📲 Get TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's official beta testing app. Download it for free from the App Store.

Already have TestFlight? Skip to step 2.

Open App Store — TestFlight
↓
2
🔗 Open the Invite Link

Tap the button below on your iPhone. This will open TestFlight and show the Aloha Rainbow beta.

Join Aloha Rainbow Beta
Note: Open this link directly on your iPhone — it won't work from a desktop browser.
↓
3
⬇️ Install the App

Tap "Install" in TestFlight to download Aloha Rainbow (version 1.0).

After installation, tap "Open" to launch the app.

↓
4
📍 Grant Permissions

Two permission prompts will appear on first launch:

  • Location — tap "Allow While Using App" to enable island auto-detection
  • Notifications — tap "Allow" to receive the morning rainbow outlook at 7am HST
Both can be changed later in Settings → Aloha Rainbow.
↓
5
🌺 Things to Try

Here's what to explore during testing:

  • Check that the app auto-detects your island (Oahu, Maui, Kauai, or Big Island)
  • Switch islands manually using the dropdown at the top
  • Read the rainbow score and time windows on the forecast map
  • Tap "🌈 I see a rainbow!" when you spot one — it pins the sighting on the map
  • Wait for the 7am morning outlook notification (Hawaii time)
🤙

You're all set — mahalo!
